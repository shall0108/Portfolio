Artist Statement

I believe that embracing processes that may appear more "disorganized" or "unusual" can lead to innovative and deeply personal expressions of art. These unique perspectives are the driving force behind my artistic work, as I explore the finer details and underlying patterns that others may overlook. In my art, I strive to push the boundaries between customary and non-conventional forms of expression, revealing the rich drapery of experiences that arise from my journey.

Most of my works involve intricate details, layered compositions, and a tendency for rapid repetition. Thisassociation with detail is not limited to visual elements but also extends to the underlying ideas and emotionswithin my art. I aim to create a sense of immersion and depth that invites viewers to engage with my work onmultiple levels. In the process of creating, I often engage in mindfulness meditation, allowing my thoughts toflow freely and emotionally. What unique thought processes shape how artists perceive and interpret the worldaround them? In what ways do personal experiences influence the creative expression of an artist's mind? How does the relationship between inner emotions and artistic creation manifest in an artist's work? What role does vulnerability play in allowing artists to explore their deepest thoughts and feelings?

This unfiltered stream of consciousness enables me to tap into authentic and instinctive expressions that Itranslate into my artistic process. In conclusion, my abstract art is a testament to the power of embracing one'sunique perspective and finding beauty in the finer details that others may overlook. By establishing a fine linebetween being an artist and being thesaurus , I can cultivate an artistic practice that is both deeply personal andsocially relevant, inviting viewers to rethink their understanding and question our reality.
